Why Muay Thai?

It's the most versatile striking art in a self-defense situation

Who benefits from Muay Thai?

Known as one of the best forms of striking in an actual self-defense situation, Muay Thai came from Thai warriors who fought in the war. Most of today’s UFC fighters choose to cross-train in Muay Thai. The sport sharpens their striking by incorporating punches, kicks, knees, and elbows. This allows fighters to be more creative and have more options in how to attack an opponent. Combining these techniques is the reason why they call the sport “the art of eight limbs.”

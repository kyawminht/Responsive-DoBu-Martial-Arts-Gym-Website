What's in our Judo program?

Our program aims to help you develop the skills you need to protect yourself in a one-on-one confrontation confidently.

What we provided?

Keep in mind that Judo, in general, is an exclusively ground-based martial art. While it’s effective in one-on-one self-defence, we recommend leaving it as a last option. If it’s possible, please flee and seek help. Our BJJ classes will provide you with the building blocks you need for a stable foundation. Each of our BJJ classes provides sparring sessions. They will allow you to put what you have learned into action in a simulated fight. They offer the experience to see how it feels with an active opponent instead of someone being a training dummy for the BJJ drills.
